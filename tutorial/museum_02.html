                                                                                ▒▒░░▓▓  ░░    ░░▒▒░░               Device No. 7 — "The TIME Machine" TIME Island Municipal Collection, acquired 1987

                                                                                ░░░░▓▓  ░░    ░░░░░░               This machine was built and operated by residents of TIME, a small island              
                                                                                ░░  ▒▒  ░░    ░░░░░░               community, and served as the island's primary device for sorting and                                               
                                                                                ░░░░▓▓  ░░    ░░░░░░               distributing mail, regulating the weekly fish market schedule, and signaling

                                                                                ░░░░▒▒  ░░    ░░░░                 collection.
