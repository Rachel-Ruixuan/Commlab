**Shanzhai New York Times** by *Rachel Li*

## Short Description
NYT itself is positioned as a serious news website, however, there are many content that is surprising to see on NYT which seems news-irrelevant and attention-baiting, while ads also takes a lot of space. Thus I found it an interesting idea of advertising through the media of news. 

## Abstract
What would a news world look like if it were entirely covered by ads? Have you ever imagined a day when you can no longer tell the difference between news and advertising? In many ways, this is already happening. If it has not yet troubled you, Almost New York Times offers a chance to experience how ads can become news (or the reverse) and what it feels like to read through advertisements for the actual news content behind. Rather than directly criticizing any specific journalism platform, the project playfully demonstrates a future where advertising becomes the dominant language of news itself.
